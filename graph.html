What This Graph Shows

Before enrichment (red dashed lines): Bernardino Luini (Q8537) and Boltraffio (Q442528) are isolated nodes — they have no connection to a teacher or art movement in Wikidata. The Baronci Altarpiece (Q1778000) floats without a museum link.

After enrichment (green solid lines): LLM-proposed triples (verified against historical sources) connect the painters to Leonardo da Vinci via P1066, to the Italian Renaissance via P135, and the altarpiece to the Pinacoteca Vaticana via P195.
